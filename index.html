Reading Your Fence's Vital Signs

Tilted posts often indicate underground issues such as frost heave, erosion, or decay.

Gaps between boards can signal wood shrinkage from improperly dried materials.

A sagging gate may be due to failing hinges or a weakening frame from moisture exposure.

Signs of splintering and cracking indicate deteriorating wood that requires timely attention.
